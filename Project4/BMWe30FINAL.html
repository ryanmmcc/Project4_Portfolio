BMW e30 Illustration


Personal project, Winter 2023, Adobe Illustrator, 11x8.5 inches.

I had begun this in late 2022, and picked it back up in early 2023. I thought that Illustrating this car would help sharpen my illustration skills. I had to choose which details I wanted to include on the car. I have found that I like to do simple designs, a skill that I was taught in my first graphic design class while in high school and has been engraved in my brain ever since. I did not want there to be too many, to the point where it looked photo-realistic, I felt that I had to remove some of the details to help put my own style on these illustrations, similar to other illustrations in the past.
